Number format[edit]

Fixed Land line +962 6 555 5555 

  +962 Jordan   6  city Amman  ..# .

Mobile phones +962 79 999 9999 (the first three numbers after 962 can be either 079, 078, 077, 075) 

   +962 Jordan   7X operator ID
