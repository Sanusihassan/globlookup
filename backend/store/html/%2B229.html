Benin telephone calling codes

Country Code: +229
International Call Prefix: 00
Trunk Prefix:

Calling formats[edit]

  • yyxx xxxx Calls within Benin
  • +229 yyxx xxxx Calls from outside Benin

The NSN length is eight digits.
