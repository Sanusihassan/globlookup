Burkina Faso telephone calling codes

Country Code: +226
International Call Prefix: 00
Trunk Prefix:

Calling formats[edit]

  • AB PQ MC DU - Calls within Burkina Faso
  • +226 AB PQ MC DU - Calls from outside Burkina Faso

The NSN length is eight digits.
