976 is the international dialing country code for Mongolia.

Numbering Convention[edit]

National significant numbers (NSN) are eight digits in length. There are two different domestic long-distance access codes in Mongolia: 01 (Mongolia Telecom network) and 02 (Mongolian Railway network).
